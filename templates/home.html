About Us

Our system helps in predicting the type of headache based on the symptoms.

Our Vision

Many people are unaware of their headaches and do not seek treatment. Headaches can have a severe negative impact on a person's professional and financial performance. It can have an adverse effect on job choices, work productivity, and family and loved ones. Our vision is to provide a solution for people who suffer from headaches on a regular basis, allowing them to determine what type of headache they have.

Our Mission

Our system assists in predicting the type of headache based on the symptoms. We will recommend available doctors in the area based on the projected headache type, as well as what patients should and shouldn't do, as well as the best exercise types for the predicted headache type.
